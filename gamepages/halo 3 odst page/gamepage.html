Halo 3: ODST

Halo 3 ODST is part of the Halo series. It is a FPS (first person shooter) game. Released on September, 25, 2009. Master Chief ends up landing on Earth and his mission is to find Cortana, who has been captured by The Prophet and The Flood. The Halo 3 campaign can be played with 3 other people on local play. This game has received a 9.5/10 on Game spot, a 94% on Metacritic, and a 9.5/10 on IGN.com. This game made up to 117 million dollars on the day that it came out.

  • Created by Bungie, Published by Microsoft Studios
  • Available for Xbox One and Xbox 360
  • First person shoot, single-player and multiplayer
