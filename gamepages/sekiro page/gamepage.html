Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

The game was released on March 22nd, 2019. This game is based on skill, not luck or power. Sekiro is an action filled game that many players enjoy playing. On opening day, Sekiro got 108,000 players on STEAM. Within 10 days of its opening, it had sold over 2 million copies. It has reviews of 90% on Metacritic, 9.5/10 on IGN.com, and a 92% on PC Gamer.

  • Developed by FromSoftware, published by Activision JP: FromSoftware
  • Available for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One
  • Single player
