Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto is part of the series of GTA. This game has one of the best storylines that gamers have ever experienced before. You can rob a bank, steal a car, commit murder, or be murdered. As long as you don’t get caught, all is allowed. Within 24 hours of it’s release, it made $800 million worldwide. It has reviews of 4.3 /5 on Common Sense Media, 9/10 on GameSpot, and a 10/10 on IGN.com.

  • Developed by Rockstar North, Published by Rockstar Games
  • Available for PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows
  • Single-player and Multiplayer
