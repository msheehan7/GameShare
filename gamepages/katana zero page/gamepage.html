Katana ZERO

This game is comparable to the game Hotline Miami. In this game, you have powers such as the ability to slow down time, dodge bullets, and dodge attacks using rolls. The point of the game is to go around killing people and racking up the most kills. This game has reviews of 83% on Metacritic, 79% on PC Gamer, and an 8.7/10 on IGN.com.

  • Developed by Akiisoft and Published by Devolver Digital
  • Available on Microsoft Windows, MacOs, and Nintendo Switch
  • Single players
