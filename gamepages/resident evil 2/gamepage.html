Resident Evil 2: Remastered

Resident Evil 2 is a remake of the 1998 version of Resident Evil 2. It has better graphics, a better story-line, and increased options and weapons. Resident Evil 2 has made over 3 million copies and demands have gone up to 4 million copies in sales. It has reviews of 91% on metacritic, 9/10 on IGN.com, 4.5/5 on PCWorld.

  • Developed by Capcom R&D Division 1, Published by Capcom
  • Available for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One
  • Single player
