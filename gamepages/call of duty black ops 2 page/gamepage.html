Call of Duty: Black Ops 2

Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 is a first-person shooter similar to the game Halo. It is part of the Call of Duty series. It features new weapons, lobbies, new multiplayer features, etc. This game made $500 million in its first 24 hours of sale. It ended up making $1 billion on its first 15 days on the market. It has reviews of 9.3/10 on IGN.com, 8/10 on GameSpot, and a 71% on PC Gamer.

  • Developed by Treyarch, published by Activision
  • Available for Microsoft Windows, Xbox 360, Playstation 3, and Wii U
  • First-person shooter, online multiplayer, local multiplayer, single-player
