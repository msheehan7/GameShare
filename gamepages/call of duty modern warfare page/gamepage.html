Call of Duty: Modern Warfare

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a game that allows the player to play a level and at the end of the level it gives the player a score based on their threat assessment. Release date is October 25th, 2019. Expected to sell over 30 million copies.

  • Developed by Infinity Ward, Published by Activision
  • Available for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One
  • Single-player, Multiplayer modes available
