Mortal Kombat 11

Mortal Kombat 11 is the ELEVENTH installment of the Mortal Kombat series. It was released in North America and Europe on May, 10th, 2019. Game critics loved the game and the story, gameplay, and improved netcode. Mortal Kombat 11 sold over 70 million copies. It had reviews of 9/10 on IGN.com, 82% on Metacritic, 85% on PC Gamer.

  • Developed by NetherRealm Studios, Published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment.
  • Available for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Google Stadia
  • Single-Player and Multiplayer
