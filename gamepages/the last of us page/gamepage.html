The Last of Us

The Last of Us is a role-playing game, based off of the choices of Ellie and Joel. The Last of Us was released on June, 14, 2013. But it was released in Japan on June, 20, 2013. Joe and Ellie have to make certain choices that will lead the player through the game. The objective of the game is to find the cure to a disease that is wiping out the human race. This game has received ratings of 5/5 on Common Sense Media, 95% on Metacritic, and a 10/10 on IGN.com. The Last of Us sold 17 million copies as of June, 14, 2018.

  • Created by Naughty Dog, Published by Sony Computer Entertainment
  • Available for Playstation 3 and Playstation 4
  • Role Playing game, Single Player
